About Us

Rotary Club Umhlatuzana is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing educational support and tutoring services to disadvantaged communities. We believe that every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their background. Our club is a breakfast club that meets between 7 and 8am on Thursday mornings. Visitors are welcome.
